Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login screen and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email.

Why is my app crashing?

App crashes can happen for several reasons. Try these steps: 1) Restart your device, 2) Update the app to the latest version, 3) Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > [App Name] > Storage > Clear Cache). If the problem persists, contact our support team with details about your device and when the crash occurs.

How do I request a refund?

Refund requests must be processed through the app store where you made the purchase. For Google Play purchases, visit play.google.com/store/account. For Microsoft Store purchases, visit account.microsoft.com/services. Our support team can guide you through the process if needed.

When will the next update be released?

We typically release updates every 2-4 weeks with new features and bug fixes. You can enable auto-update in your app store settings to always have the latest version. Follow us on social media for announcements about upcoming features.
